Life insurance that's all about life

(not the bit that comes afterwards)

It's life insurance, but not as you know it.

Say hello to Integrity.

At Integrity, we aim to prove that life insurance doesn't have to be complex, and that the right policy can make a big difference throughout life (not just afterwards).

By keeping things clear and simple, we're here to help Australians understand their life insurance options, get the right cover, and make claims hassle-free.

What makes us different?

We keep it simple.

With our straight-forward, flexible products and clear explanations, we make it easy for you to find the right policy for your stage of life and circumstances.

You're kept in the loop.

Whether you're a customer or an adviser, you shouldn't be kept guessing, so we communicate openly at every stage.

We do what's right for the customer.

(not what's most convenient for us). That means looking for ways to help, rather than finding excuses not to.

Making claims shouldn't be like pulling teeth

There's no point having life insurance if you can't make claims when you need to. So at Integrity, we've worked hard to make the claims process hassle-free – and we actively look for ways to pay your claim (rather than excuses not to). We also keep communication open, so you're never left hanging.
