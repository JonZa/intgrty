It’s life insurance, but not as you know it.

Say hello to Integrity.
Life insurance, as it’s meant to be.

Integrity Life was created out of a desire to fundamentally disrupt the industry and improve life insurance in Australia. By blending our expertise with life insurance and technology we are creating products that are easy to understand and simple to use.

Integrity is a life insurer that is not only experienced and capable but also one that can be trusted to bring new levels of service to the industry. We think the time has come to do things a little differently.

We’re a team of knowledgeable people who are genuinely here to help because when times get tough, that’s what you need.

What makes us different?

We do what’s right for you (not what’s most convenient for us). This means looking for ways to help, rather than finding excuses not to.

We keep it simple. Easy to understand and tailor made products.

We look at things from end to end. Insurance that not only covers people for today but tomorrow as well, through every stage of life.

You’re kept in the loop. As an adviser, you shouldn’t be kept guessing, so we communicate openly at every stage.

We’re people too. Sometimes all you need is someone on the other end to give you with the help you need.

We're all about support. Support during application, claims and everything in between.

We like to share. Leveraging our knowledge, expertise and relationships to support you and your business.

Backed by real experience. Team of experts with a wealth of knowledge and experience to draw on.

Everything we do is done with integrity, transparency and respect. These are our values, they are how we measure ourselves. It is why we are called Integrity.

On your side, and by your side.

Claims time is when you find out who really makes the process quick, simple and easy – and it’s when you’ll see how much Integrity is willing to do to help. From the start, we’ll take charge of the process, doing the heavy lifting so you can get on with what you do best, looking after your clients.

With Integrity, claims are never treated as a transaction – they are given the care and respect they deserve from start to finish. We look for every opportunity to pay claims, and if we can’t, we’re straight up about the reasons why. We manage all personal information with care, and we assign a dedicated person to you, so you’re dealing with someone who knows you and knows your case.

Whatever happens, you can rest assured that with Integrity, we have your clients’ best interests at the heart of everything we do.

How can we help?

We’d love to work with you to give your clients a better life insurance experience including:

  • A great range of products designed for their changing needs.
  • Fair pricing and clear communication.
  • And we’re always here to help.
