Our investors

Independent and privately owned by local and offshore investors, Integrity has access to more than $180 million in capital, comprising equity and debt. Our investors include four funds under the discretionary management of London-based firm Leadenhall Capital Partners, which is a subsidiary of Mitsui Sumitomo Insurance Group, and long-established Japanese life insurer Daido Life Insurance Group.
